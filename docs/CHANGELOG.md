# Vision

LAW.066.city is a modern legal platform designed to connect individuals and businesses with trusted legal professionals through an intelligent, elegant and highly accessible digital experience.

Rather than becoming another lawyer directory, LAW.066.city aims to become the leading legal search platform in the Arab world.

The platform serves as the legal vertical of the 066 Platform ecosystem.

---

# Mission

To make legal services:

- Easier to Find
- Easier to Trust
- Easier to Access

through modern technology and exceptional user experience.

# Platform Principles

## Arabic First

Arabic is the primary language of the platform.

Every feature shall be designed in Arabic before English.

English remains fully supported.

---

## Search First

Search is the heart of LAW.066.city.

Users should naturally search for:

- Lawyers
- Legal services
- Cities
- Practice areas
- Legal articles
- Legal topics

Future versions will evolve toward AI-assisted legal search.

---

## Design First

Every screen must answer one question:

> "Can the user immediately understand what to do?"

Visual elegance must never reduce usability.

---

# Theme Engine

Every page and every component shall support:

- Light Theme
- Dark Theme
- System Theme

No hardcoded colors are allowed.

All components must consume semantic design tokens.

Example:

```css
background: var(--surface);
color: var(--text-primary);
```
