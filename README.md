# LAW.066.city

> **مستشارك القانوني**  
> **أقرب إليك**

A modern legal technology platform connecting individuals and businesses with trusted legal professionals through an intelligent, elegant and accessible digital experience.

---

## Vision

LAW.066.city is not another lawyer directory.

Our vision is to build the leading legal search platform in the Arab world by combining exceptional user experience with modern software engineering.

LAW.066.city is the first product in the **066 Platform** ecosystem.

---

## Mission

Make legal services:

- Easier to Find
- Easier to Trust
- Easier to Access
